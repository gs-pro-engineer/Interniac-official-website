Our Mission

Interniac is a marketplace that supports high schoolers and empowers businesses in hiring bright students.

  • Student benefits
  • Employer benefits

Interniac serves as a marketplace for internships where students can shop for internships that interest them. Students are able to create their own profile that turns into a standardized resume when applying for the job. They also have access to a bi-weekly newsletter with career spotlights and professional development resources.

Interniac is the one-stop-shop for employers looking to build successful and hassle-free internship programs. Employers can create a job description, view candidates’ profiles, and hire the perfect intern directly on our platform. Companies should focus solely on mentoring and extracting value from their interns both before and after the internship, and let us handle all the logistics.
